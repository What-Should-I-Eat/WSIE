About Us

About the Site

Welcome to What Should I Eat—your go-to culinary companion designed to cater to your unique dietary needs and preferences. Whether you're managing food allergies, adhering to specific restrictions, or simply seeking fresh meal ideas, our platform makes cooking effortless and enjoyable.

With an intuitive interface and a comprehensive recipe database, you'll always find dishes that delight your taste buds while meeting your dietary goals. Our advanced search engine allows you to customize results based on ingredients, restrictions, and even calorie counts.

What Should I Eat is more than just a recipe finder—it's a thriving community of food enthusiasts. Here, you can exchange tips, share experiences, and inspire others. Rate recipes, suggest substitutions, or upload your own culinary creations to help others on their journey.

Whether you're seeking inspiration for a quick dinner, a healthy snack, or a festive feast, our community is here to guide you every step of the way. Dive into a world of flavor with us and make every meal an exciting and healthy adventure!

Our Story

What Should I Eat is a project driven by graduate students at Drexel University's College of Computing and Informatics. It was founded by Torie Thacker, who faced challenges finding diverse, customizable recipes that aligned with her unique dietary restrictions. Recognizing the lack of online resources that met her needs, Torie developed What Should I Eat as her graduate capstone project in the fall of 2023.

Since its inception, the project has evolved to address a wide range of dietary restrictions through the dedication of current Drexel students completing their capstone project. Each academic quarter, new features are introduced to enhance the platform's functionality, guided by Torie's vision.

We value your input! If you have ideas for new features, visit the Contact Us tab to share your suggestions. To learn more about the talented students behind the site, explore the section below.
